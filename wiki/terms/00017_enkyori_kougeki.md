# 遠距離攻撃 (enkyori_kougeki)

**ranged attack** · えんきょりこうげき · enkyori_kougeki

an attack performed at range, without physical contact with the target

## Orthography notes

遠距離: 遠 (far) + 距離 (distance). 攻撃: attack. The compound is descriptive, not proprietary. Goo dictionary lists 遠距離 as a standard word. Fire Emblem uses the 遠距離 vs. 近接 distinction as a core weapon-type concept.

## Implicit meanings

Contrasts structurally with 近接攻撃 (melee attack). 遠距離 (far distance) is a general Japanese term used in transportation, telecommunications, and sports as well as games.

## Differences across game settings

Cross-genre applicable. The docx confirms this term has independent Fire Emblem precedent. In miniature wargames, applies to shooting weapons. Not WH-specific.

## Usage

**Japanese:** {遠|えん}{距|きょ}{離|り}{攻|こう}{撃|げき}を{行|おこな}う (perform a ranged attack); {遠|えん}{距|きょ}{離|り}{武|ぶ}{器|き} (ranged weapon)
**English:** "ranged attack", "ranged strike", "shooting attack".

## Comparative notes

近接攻撃 is the antonym. In Fire Emblem, 遠距離 weapon vs. 近接 weapon is a fundamental dichotomy. In miniature wargames, 遠距離攻撃 happens in the 射撃段階.

## Examples

> {射|しゃ}{撃|げき}{段|だん}{階|かい}に{遠|えん}{距|きょ}{離|り}{攻|こう}{撃|げき}を{実|じっ}{行|こう}する。
> Execute a ranged attack during the shooting phase.
