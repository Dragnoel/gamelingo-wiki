# 射撃段階 (shageki_dankai)

**shooting phase** · しゃげきだんかい · shageki_dankai

the phase of a turn in which units with ranged weapons may fire at enemy targets

## Orthography notes

射撃: 射 (shoot/arrow) + 撃 (strike). Attested in the Goo dictionary as a military/ballistics term. 段階 replaces フェイズ to form a distinct compound.

## Implicit meanings

射撃 (shageki, shooting/firing) is a standard Japanese military term used in military ballistics and JWC documentation. It is not a WH coinage. The risk is the 射撃フェイズ compound, which is the exact WH40K Japan phase name.

## Differences across game settings

High-priority WH flag per the docx. 射撃フェイズ is the direct WH40K/AoS Japan term (AoS 4th ed. later changed it to 遠隔フェイズ for ranged phase). 射撃段階 is the recommended safe alternative.

## Usage

**Japanese:** {射|しゃ}{撃|げき}{段|だん}{階|かい}に{遠|えん}{距|きょ}{離|り}{攻|こう}{撃|げき}を{行|おこな}う (perform ranged attacks during the shooting phase)
**English:** "shooting phase" or "ranged phase".

## Comparative notes

射撃フェイズ is high-priority WH. 射撃段階 achieves the same meaning. AoS Japan's 遠隔フェイズ (remote/ranged phase) is a third option but carries a different WH association.

## Examples

> {射|しゃ}{撃|げき}{段|だん}{階|かい}に{遠|えん}{距|きょ}{離|り}{攻|こう}{撃|げき}を{宣|せん}{言|げん}する。
> Declare a ranged attack in the shooting phase.
