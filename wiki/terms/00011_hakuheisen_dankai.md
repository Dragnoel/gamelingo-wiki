# 白兵戦段階 (hakuheisen_dankai)

**fighting phase / close combat phase** · はくへいせんだんかい · hakuheisen_dankai

the phase of a turn in which units in base contact or engagement range fight in melee

## Orthography notes

白兵戦: 白兵 (bare/white weapons = sword and spear, as opposed to firearms) + 戦 (battle). A standard military Japanese compound. 段階 replaces フェイズ. WH AoS Japan 4th ed. uses 近接フェイズ as an alternative WH term; 白兵戦段階 avoids both.

## Implicit meanings

白兵戦 (hakuheisen, hand-to-hand combat) is a classical Japanese military term used in WWII-era orders, military history texts, and the Goo dictionary. The term predates Warhammer. The WH concern is specifically 白兵戦フェイズ as a compound.

## Differences across game settings

High-priority WH flag. 白兵戦フェイズ is the WH40K Japan term. 近接格闘段階 is the fully neutral alternative if 白兵戦 is also considered too close in context.

## Usage

**Japanese:** {白|はく}{兵|へい}{戦|せん}{段|だん}{階|かい}に{近|きん}{接|せつ}{攻|こう}{撃|げき}を{行|おこな}う (perform melee attacks in the fighting phase)
**English:** "fighting phase" or "close combat phase".

## Comparative notes

白兵戦フェイズ is high-priority WH. 白兵戦段階 is the recommended safe form. 近接格闘段階 is fully neutral if extra caution is needed.

## Examples

> {白|はく}{兵|へい}{戦|せん}{段|だん}{階|かい}が{始|はじ}まった。
> The fighting phase has begun.
