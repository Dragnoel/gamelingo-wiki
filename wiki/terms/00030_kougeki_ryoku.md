# 攻撃力 (kougeki_ryoku)

**offense / attack power** · こうげきりょく · kougeki_ryoku

a stat representing the offensive power or damage output of a unit

## Orthography notes

攻撃力: 攻撃 (attack) + 力 (power/capacity). Universal Japanese game term. Fire Emblem uses 攻撃力 (ATK) as a core stat. Weblio dictionary lists it as a general game/military term. Not WH-specific.

## Implicit meanings

力 (ryoku) as a stat suffix signals a capacity or power level. 攻撃力 is the standard JRPG and SRPG term for the ATK stat.

## Differences across game settings

Universal across all Japanese game genres. Fire Emblem uses 攻撃力 as the ATK stat. In miniature wargames, may appear as a damage value or wound roll modifier.

## Usage

**Japanese:** {攻|こう}{撃|げき}{力|りょく}ステータス (ATK stat); {攻|こう}{撃|げき}{力|りょく}が{高|たか}いユニット (high attack-power unit)
**English:** "attack power", "offense", "ATK stat".

## Comparative notes

防御 (bougyo, defense) is the antonym stat. 攻撃数 (kougeki-suu) counts attacks; 攻撃力 measures how effective each attack is.

## Examples

> {攻|こう}{撃|げき}{力|りょく}が{防|ぼう}{御|ぎょ}{値|ち}を{超|こ}えた{場|ば}{合|あい}、ダメージが{発|はっ}{生|せい}する。
> Damage occurs when attack power exceeds the defense value.
