# 近接攻撃 (kinsetsu_kougeki)

**melee attack / contact attack** · きんせつこうげき · kinsetsu_kougeki

an attack performed in physical contact or at very short range with the target

## Orthography notes

近接: 近 (near) + 接 (contact/touch). 攻撃: attack. Fire Emblem uses the 近接 vs. 遠距離 weapon distinction as a core mechanic. JWC documentation attests 近接 in wargame contexts. Not WH-specific.

## Implicit meanings

近接 (close/adjacent) is a widely-used Japanese word across gaming, military, and everyday language. The compound is descriptive and not proprietary.

## Differences across game settings

In miniature wargames, 近接攻撃 is resolved during the 白兵戦段階. In video games (SRPGs), 近接武器 (melee weapon) is a standard category.

## Usage

**Japanese:** {近|きん}{接|せつ}{攻|こう}{撃|げき}を{宣|せん}{言|げん}する (declare a melee attack); {近|きん}{接|せつ}{武|ぶ}{器|き} (melee weapon)
**English:** "melee attack", "close combat attack", "contact attack".

## Comparative notes

遠距離攻撃 is the antonym. In Fire Emblem, 近接 and 遠距離 are the two primary weapon-range categories. In miniature wargames, 近接攻撃 occurs in the 白兵戦段階.

## Examples

> {白|はく}{兵|へい}{戦|せん}{段|だん}{階|かい}に{近|きん}{接|せつ}{攻|こう}{撃|げき}を{実|じっ}{行|こう}する。
> Execute a melee attack during the fighting phase.
