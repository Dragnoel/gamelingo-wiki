# 突撃段階 (totsugeki_dankai)

**charging phase** · とつげきだんかい · totsugeki_dankai

the phase of a turn in which units may declare and execute charges against enemy units

## Orthography notes

突撃段階 uses 段階 (dankai, step/stage) in place of フェイズ to create a distinct compound that avoids the WH Japan phase-naming pattern. 突撃 is a standard military Japanese compound: 突 (thrust/rush) + 撃 (strike).

## Implicit meanings

突撃 (charge/rush) is a classical Japanese military command term used in WWII-era orders and historical writing. The component words are not proprietary; only the specific compound 突撃フェイズ mirrors WH Japan's branded vocabulary.

## Differences across game settings

Miniature wargame specific. The docx explicitly recommends 突撃段階 over 突撃フェイズ for this reason. 突撃 is attested in the Goo dictionary as a general military term.

## Usage

**Japanese:** {突|とつ}{撃|げき}{段|だん}{階|かい}に{突|とつ}{撃|げき}を{宣|せん}{言|げん}する (declare a charge in the charging phase)
**English:** "charging phase" or "charge phase".

## Comparative notes

突撃フェイズ is the WH40K/AoS Japan term and should be avoided to prevent IP ambiguity. 突撃段階 achieves the same meaning with a clearly distinct compound.

## Examples

> {突|とつ}{撃|げき}{段|だん}{階|かい}を{開|かい}{始|し}する。
> Begin the charging phase.
