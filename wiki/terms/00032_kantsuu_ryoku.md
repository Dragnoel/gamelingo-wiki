# 貫通力 (kantsuu_ryoku)

**penetration / armour penetration** · かんつうりょく · kantsuu_ryoku

a stat representing a weapon's ability to pierce through a target's armour

## Orthography notes

貫通: 貫 (pierce through) + 通 (pass through). 力 (ryoku): power/capacity as a stat suffix. 貫通 is a standard Japanese word for penetration (Goo dictionary). 貫通力 appears in military ballistics contexts independently of WH Japan. The WH Japan term is specifically 貫通値 (penetration value).

## Implicit meanings

⚠️ WH: 貫通値 is the WH40K Japan term for Armour Penetration (AP). The 値 suffix is WH's datasheet-value naming convention. 貫通力 uses the 力 suffix (capability/power) instead, avoiding the WH-specific form while retaining the same concept.

## Differences across game settings

⚠️ WH high-priority: 貫通値 is the exact WH40K Japan AP stat label. Recommended form: 貫通力 (penetrating power) to clearly distinguish from WH datasheet terminology.

## Usage

**Japanese:** {貫|かん}{通|つう}{力|りょく}ステータス (penetration stat); {高|たか}い{貫|かん}{通|つう}{力|りょく}を{持|も}つ{武|ぶ}{器|き} (weapon with high penetration)
**English:** "armour penetration", "penetration stat", "AP stat".

## Comparative notes

貫通値 is WH Japan's specific term and should be avoided. 貫通力 achieves the same concept. 鎧通し is a historical flavour option suitable for non-stat flavour text.

## Examples

> {銃|じゅう}{弾|だん}はヘルメットを{貫|かん}{通|つう}した。
> A bullet pierced the helmet.
