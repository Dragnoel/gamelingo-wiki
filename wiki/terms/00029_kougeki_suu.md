# 攻撃数 (kougeki_suu)

**attacks (stat) / number of attacks** · こうげきすう · kougeki_suu

a stat indicating how many attack dice or attack attempts a unit makes per combat

## Orthography notes

攻撃数: 攻撃 (attack) + 数 (number/count). Descriptive compound. WH Japan uses 攻 as a stat abbreviation on datasheets, not the full word. The full compound 攻撃数 is generic.

## Implicit meanings

The 数 (suu, number/count) suffix creates a stat measuring the quantity of attacks. WH Japan abbreviates 攻撃数 as 攻 on datasheets, but the full compound is generic.

## Differences across game settings

Miniature wargame and TRPG specific. Appears on unit stat profiles. Distinct from 攻撃力 (attack power — how effective each attack is).

## Usage

**Japanese:** {攻|こう}{撃|げき}{数|すう}3（{1|いち}{ターン|たーん}に3{回|かい}{攻|こう}{撃|げき}できる）
**English:** "Attacks: 3" on a stat profile; "number of attacks".

## Comparative notes

攻撃力 (kougeki-ryoku) measures attack effectiveness or damage; 攻撃数 measures how many attacks are made. Both appear on unit profiles.

## Examples

> このユニットの{攻|こう}{撃|げき}{数|すう}は3だ。
> This unit's Attacks stat is 3.
