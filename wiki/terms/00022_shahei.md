# 遮蔽 (shahei)

**cover / hindering terrain** · しゃへい · shahei

terrain or a unit that screens or obstructs, providing a defensive bonus to units behind it

## Orthography notes

遮蔽: 遮 (block/obstruct) + 蔽 (cover/conceal). Classical Japanese architectural and military term. Goo dictionary lists it as a standard word. The WH concern is the specific compound 遮蔽物ボーナス, not 遮蔽 itself. 障害 is the fully neutral alternative if caution is preferred.

## Implicit meanings

⚠️ WH*: 遮蔽 appears in WH40K Japan as 遮蔽物 (cover object) and 遮蔽物ボーナス (cover bonus). However, 遮蔽 is a standard Japanese architectural and military term meaning to screen/obstruct, predating Warhammer by centuries.

## Differences across game settings

⚠️ WH*: Use with awareness that 遮蔽物 compounds appear in WH Japan. 遮蔽 alone is classical and low-risk. 障害 (obstacle) is the fully neutral alternative used in JWC terrain rules.

## Usage

**Japanese:** {遮|しゃ}{蔽|へい}を{利|り}{用|よう}する (use cover); {遮|しゃ}{蔽|へい}ボーナス (cover bonus)
**English:** "cover" (as in 'in cover'), "hindering terrain", "screening".

## Comparative notes

遮蔽物ボーナス is the WH Japan compound; 遮蔽 alone is classical. 障害 (shougai, obstacle) is the recommended fully neutral alternative that avoids any WH association.

## Examples

> {遮|しゃ}{蔽|へい}に{隠|かく}れたユニットは{防|ぼう}{御|ぎょ}ボーナスを{得|え}る。
> Units in cover gain a defensive bonus.
