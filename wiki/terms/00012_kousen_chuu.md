# 交戦中 (kousen_chuu)

**engaged in combat** · こうせんちゅう · kousen_chuu

a status indicating that a unit is currently engaged in close combat with an enemy unit

## Orthography notes

交戦 (kousen): 交 (mutual/interact) + 戦 (battle). 中 (chuu) as a suffix: in the state of. A completely standard compound. JWC glossary attests combat engagement terms independently.

## Implicit meanings

中 functions as a continuous-state suffix (〜している状態). 交戦中 is a standard Japanese diplomatic and military term meaning 'at war / engaged in combat'. Kotobank cites it in diplomatic/military context, independent of any game system.

## Differences across game settings

In miniature wargames, 交戦中 is a game state (being in base contact or engagement range with an enemy). Distinguished from the WH-specific 接敵状態, which should be avoided.

## Usage

**Japanese:** {交|こう}{戦|せん}{中|ちゅう}の{ユニット|ゆにっと}は{移|い}{動|どう}できない (engaged units cannot move)
**English:** "engaged", "in combat", "engaged in close combat".

## Comparative notes

接敵状態 (setteki joutai) is the WH Japan game status term and must be avoided. 交戦中 is a classical Japanese term safe for all uses.

## Examples

> {交|こう}{戦|せん}{中|ちゅう}のユニットは{突|とつ}{撃|げき}{段|だん}{階|かい}に{移|い}{動|どう}できない。
> Engaged units cannot move during the charging phase.
