# 攻撃 (kougeki)

**attack** · こうげき · kougeki

an offensive action directed at an enemy unit or target

## Orthography notes

攻撃: 攻 (attack/assault) + 撃 (strike/hit). Standard Japanese military and game term. Goo dictionary entry is generic. JWC glossary uses 攻撃作戦 (attack operation). Fire Emblem and Famicom Wars use 攻撃 as a core mechanic term. Not WH-specific.

## Implicit meanings

攻撃 is the general term covering all offensive actions. When specificity is required, 遠距離攻撃 (ranged) or 近接攻撃 (melee) are used.

## Differences across game settings

Universal across all game genres. WH Japan abbreviates it as 攻 (attack stat shorthand) on datasheets, but the full word 攻撃 is entirely generic.

## Usage

**Japanese:** {攻|こう}{撃|げき}する (to attack); {攻|こう}{撃|げき}を{宣|せん}{言|げん}する (declare an attack); {攻|こう}{撃|げき}{力|りょく} (attack stat)
**English:** "attack" as noun or verb.

## Comparative notes

攻撃 is the umbrella term. Use 遠距離攻撃 or 近接攻撃 when the attack type must be specified. 攻撃力 (attack power) is the stat form.

## Examples

> {攻|こう}{撃|げき}は{最|さい}{大|だい}の{防|ぼう}{衛|えい}。
> Attack is the best form of defense.
