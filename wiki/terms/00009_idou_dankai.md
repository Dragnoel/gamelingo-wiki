# 移動段階 (idou_dankai)

**moving phase** · いどうだんかい · idou_dankai

the phase of a turn in which units may move on the battlefield

## Orthography notes

移動 (idou): 移 (transfer/shift) + 動 (move). 段階 replaces フェイズ to avoid the WH40K Japan phase-naming pattern. JWC documentation uses 移動作戦 (movement operations) as independent evidence for 移動 in wargame contexts.

## Implicit meanings

移動 (movement) is a completely generic Japanese word used in JWC wargame documentation, Fire Emblem (移動力 = movement stat), and everyday language. The WH concern is the specific 移動フェイズ compound, not the word 移動 itself.

## Differences across game settings

Standard phase in miniature wargames. WH40K Japan uses 移動フェイズ; using 移動段階 clearly distinguishes this project's terminology.

## Usage

**Japanese:** {移|い}{動|どう}{段|だん}{階|かい}に{ユニット|ゆにっと}を{動|うご}かす (move units in the moving phase)
**English:** "moving phase" or "movement phase".

## Comparative notes

移動フェイズ is the WH40K Japan term; lower IP risk than 突撃フェイズ but still a WH compound pattern. 移動段階 is recommended.

## Examples

> {移|い}{動|どう}{段|だん}{階|かい}に{前|ぜん}{進|しん}する。
> Advance during the moving phase.
