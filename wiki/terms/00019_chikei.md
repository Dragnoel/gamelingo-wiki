# 地形 (chikei)

**terrain** · ちけい · chikei

terrain features on the battlefield that affect movement and combat

## Orthography notes

地形: 地 (ground/earth) + 形 (form/shape). Classical military geography term. JWC documentation section is titled '地形と移動' (terrain and movement). Not WH-specific. テレイン is the WH Japan term for physical model components specifically.

## Implicit meanings

地形 is a classical Japanese military and geographic term predating all wargames. The docx notes that WH Japan uses テレイン for physical terrain model pieces and 地形 for terrain type — a useful distinction to preserve.

## Differences across game settings

Standard wargame term. 地形 refers to terrain type and rules. テレイン (from English 'terrain') is WH Japan's term for the physical model pieces used to represent terrain. Using 地形 for terrain rules and types avoids the WH-specific usage.

## Usage

**Japanese:** {地|ち}{形|けい}{ルール|るーる} (terrain rules); {地|ち}{形|けい}の{効|こう}{果|か} (terrain effect); {困|こん}{難|なん}{地|ち}{形|けい} (difficult terrain)
**English:** "terrain" as a rulebook category.

## Comparative notes

テレイン is WH Japan's term for terrain model components. 地形 covers terrain rules, types, and effects — the broader and more appropriate term for general use.

## Examples

> {地|ち}{形|けい}は{移|い}{動|どう}と{射|しゃ}{線|せん}に{影|えい}{響|きょう}する。
> Terrain affects movement and line of sight.
