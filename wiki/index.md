# Gaming Language Wiki

A reference for the language of play in board games, video games, and TTRPGs, with focus on Japanese↔English comparative usage. Covers implicit meanings, orthographic choices (kanji vs. katakana vs. hiragana), and how the same concept shifts across game settings and genres.

Use the **search bar** (top right) to find any term in Japanese or English.

## About

Entries are sourced from rulebooks, game documentation, academic references, and dictionary evidence. Each entry documents register, domain (board / video / TTRPG / card), orthographic choice, and comparative JP↔EN usage. Furigana readings are verified against Jisho; example sentences are sourced from Tatoeba where available.
